Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retrieer or simply Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog.It was in the United Kingdom from fishing dogs imported from the colony of Newfoundland, and was names after Labrador region of the colony.

  2. Swimming
  3. Social Gatherings
